Which start and stop stations are most popular?

Below are some visualizations describing where the most popular locations are, and what are the stations. This was done by finding the number of trips for each unique station. This can be done by grouping by station, and aggregating by count. The most popular stations are in the heart of the financial district, and Little Tokyo. Broadway & 3rd is our top Starting Station and 7th & Flower is our top ending station.
