Where did our data come from?

The data came from https://bikeshare.metro.net/about/data/ . We used data on Downtown LA Bike trips provided by this source, and given to us by Capital One. The data we had wasn't super useful to learn about specific location names (it just had Station IDs), so we added all the actual locations for each Station ID. We used this dataframe in Jupyter Notebook for all our visualizations and calculations.
